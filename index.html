The Scholarship Jacket

Based on the story by Martha Salinas

A Texas School's Greatest Honor…
The gold & green Scholarship Jacket — given to the top student!
Martha's Dream
Straight A's since 1st grade… That jacket will be MINE!
Martha Overhears the Teachers
"Give the jacket to Joann — Martha's Mexican!"
"I REFUSE! Her grades are the best!"
The Principal's Bad News
"The Board says the jacket costs $15 this year. You must pay."
My heart completely sank…
Grandpa's Wisdom in the Bean Field
"If I pay for it, then it wouldn't really be a scholarship jacket, Grandpa."
Grandpa smiled — and sent her back to school.
The Principal Relents — Martha Gets Her Jacket! 🎉
"Okay, damn it. We'll make an exception. You'll get your jacket!"
She ran home — crying, laughing, and whistling a silly tune. 🎶
